Lead Scoring System

Using Claude Code, I developed a machine learning solution that identifies which leads to filter, delivering $1.4M+ guaranteed annual ROI through operational efficiency.

The Challenge

In my role at an EdTech company, I identified a critical inefficiency: our sales team processed hundreds of thousands of annual leads with an enrollment rate of less than 3%. This meant massive effort spent on leads unlikely to convert.

Rather than traditional lead prioritization, I asked a different question: What if we could confidently identify which leads NOT to pursue?

The "Don't Engage" Innovation

The breakthrough was reframing the problem. Instead of prioritizing leads, we identify the low-probability leads to safely ignore.

How It Works

The XGBoost model analyzes lead characteristics like enrollment intent, timing preferences, marketing channel, and engagement patterns to predict conversion probability. Leads scoring below a carefully calibrated threshold are filtered from sales outreach.

Risk Management: We set conservative thresholds (95%+ precision) and validated that 25% of "missed" enrollments happen through other channels anyway, reducing true risk to just 3.5%.

Business Value

  • Conservative Scenario: $1.4M AUD/year from time savings alone
  • Realistic Scenario: $2.0M - $2.5M AUD/year as sales focuses on quality leads
  • Strong Performance: $4M+ AUD/year with improved conversion rates
