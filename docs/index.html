About

My background spans digital media, customer experience, and analytics. These days I spend most of my time working on practical systems that improve decisions, reduce manual work, and make outcomes easier to measure.

This is a working portfolio first. It is intentionally transparent about what is complete, what is still in progress, and what I am learning along the way.

How I Work

A lightweight structure I use to keep projects grounded and useful.

1. Clarify the Question

Make the decision explicit, map constraints, and define what a good outcome would look like.

2. Compare Options

Use data, experiments, and structured tradeoffs to evaluate options before committing to one path.

3. Ship, Measure, Reflect

Deliver incrementally, track what changed, and use the learning to improve the next iteration.
