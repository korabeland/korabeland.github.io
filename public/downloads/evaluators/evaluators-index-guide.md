## How to Use Evaluators

1. Generate your document using the corresponding production template
2. Copy your document and the relevant evaluator prompt into Claude
3. The evaluator will score your document across 5-8 custom dimensions (0-5 scale)
4. Review the SHIP/REVISE/REJECT verdict and actionable fixes
5. Revise and re-evaluate if needed
