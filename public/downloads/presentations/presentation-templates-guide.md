# Presentation Templates Guide

This folder contains production-ready prompts for creating high-impact presentations.

## Files in This Folder

- **product_launch_presentation.md** - Template for creating compelling product launch presentations with clear narrative arcs, proof points, and audience engagement strategies.
- **product_roadmap_presentation.md** - Template for communicating product vision and roadmap to stakeholders with clear prioritization and timeline messaging.
- **pitch_deck_quality_evaluator.md** - Quality evaluation framework for assessing pitch decks on dimensions including Hook Strength, Clarity of Value Prop, Proof Density, and CTA Clarity.

## How to Use

1. Select the appropriate presentation type (launch or roadmap)
2. Use the template to generate your presentation outline and content
3. Evaluate your deck with **pitch_deck_quality_evaluator.md** to ensure persuasive impact

## Key Principle

Presentations are **decision catalysts**. Every slide should move the audience closer to a specific action or understanding.
