COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE REQUIREMENTS:

• Accurate competitive information from reliable sources (websites, analyst reports, customer feedback)

• Up-to-date pricing, product features, and market positioning based on recent research

• Win/loss analysis integration showing real-world competitive dynamics and deal outcomes

• Customer perspective on competitive alternatives with specific feedback and decision criteria

• Sales team input on competitive scenarios, objections, and successful positioning strategies



SALES ENABLEMENT FOCUS:

• **Positioning Messages:** Clear, memorable differentiators that resonate with target customers

• **Proof Points:** Specific examples, case studies, and validation for competitive claims

• **Objection Handling:** Scripted responses to common competitive objections and concerns

• **Discovery Questions:** Sales questions that uncover customer needs favoring our solution

• **Competitive Scenarios:** Guidance on when to engage competitively vs when to avoid



COMPETITOR ANALYSIS DEPTH:

• **Direct Competitors:** Feature-for-feature comparison, pricing analysis, customer overlap

• **Indirect Competitors:** Alternative solutions, different approaches, market substitutes

• **Emerging Threats:** New entrants, technology shifts, market disruptions

• **Partner Conflicts:** Channel partners who also compete, ecosystem competitive dynamics



ACTIONABLE SALES GUIDANCE:

• **Ideal Customer Profile:** Customer characteristics where we win vs each competitor

• **Differentiation Messaging:** Key messages that highlight our advantages effectively

• **Proof Point Library:** Customer stories, metrics, validation that support our positioning

• **Competitive Landmines:** Competitor strengths to avoid highlighting or engaging directly

• **Reference Customers:** Specific customers won from each competitor with permission to reference
